KANGNEKO — 강네코
Hi — ! I’m a video director and photographer based in Seoul, with an interest in experimental visuals, narrative structures, and multi-format work. My work explores the intersection of analog and digital — where contrasting textures meet to create something unexpected and new.
I currently lead 'NEITHER.I', a creative team producing AI-based artwork, together with Ypnida. We’ve worked on AI VFX and AI graphics for various K-POP artists' music videos, pushing the boundaries of what’s visually possible with machine learning and generative AI.
At the core, we focus on using AI to explore new visual languages and experimental forms.
